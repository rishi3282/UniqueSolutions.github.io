Coco Pith Blocks

The main constraint in maintaining level of organic matter in soil is non-availability of the requisite quantity of organic matter. Environmental protection through development of clean technologies and bioremediation of unes and environmental pollutants is one of the main areas of biotechnology.

  1. A substance that has the ability to retain moisture and can be used to promote plant health.
  2. Has a water storage capacity of about 18 times its weight and soil erosion instead of wasting water.
  3. Due to its light weight, it is very suitable for vertical gardening, mulberry crops, of top gardening or any other gardening in any place, where it is foreign and cannot add excess weight.
  4. Plants grow healthily as the electrical conductivity is less than 0,5. Lignin is low and has a pll of 6.5 se these are all positive factors for healthy plant growth.
  5. Once inhaled, it does not soak for a long time for about 30 days as it has the ability to retain moisture.
  6. Due to its light weight, it is suitable for plant air transport and other transport within 3 days use of coco pith the seeds will germinate.
