Products Description

Vermicompost

Why the soil should be fertilized There are many types of land, for example red soil, sandy soil, rocky soil and calcareous soils. Creatures are more problematic to live in any soil.
Cultivation of crops means that the land must survive and crops must be grown on the surviving land. Soil survival means the photosynthesis of microorganisms living in the soil, which means that plants need organic matter to contain the bacteria that can be used to make food through metabolism.
In most troubled parts of the world there is a shortage of this organic matter, in such areas there is a need to cultivate crops that contain organic matter vermicompost.
Uses of making vermicompost:

1) The main objective is to compost the ecological, livestock waste and decomposing material without wasting it anywhere.
2) Income generation and employment creation factor
3)The surroundings are kept clean by using all the garbage to make vermicompost.

An important vermicompost preparation process:
Earthworm manure can be fertilized faster with the help of earthworms such as non-borrowed Eisenia fetida and Eudrilides. The process is environmentally friendly, without harming the environment.

Burning and incineration of waste products into the sea, rivers, and canals releases hazardous carbon dioxide, carbon monoxide gases, and some toxic gases into the environment.

Safe waste disposal is the cycle of turning leaves, plants and plants back into waste, consuming then, converting them into energy, converting them into organic manure and using them to grow the same plants. Reaching the land where a plant, leaf, or fruit originated from an earth is a cycle of safe waste disposal.

Vermicompost can be very useful in stopping the process as it is a nuisance for nature to burn waste incorrectly, bury their seas ashes and put them in plastic covers wherever they can be disposed of.

This vermicompost is for whole food plants with all kinds of nutrients at 6.5 pH that can be easily and in the form of odorless pellets.

**Our product is exclusively made from cow and buffalo dung**
